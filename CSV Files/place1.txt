The Eiffel Tower is one of the most famous landmarks in the world. Located in Paris, France, it was constructed in 1889 as the entrance arch to the Worldâ€™s Fair. Designed by Gustave Eiffel, the tower stands about 330 meters tall. Originally criticized by many artists and intellectuals, it is now a beloved symbol of France. Visitors can climb the tower or take an elevator to the top. The view of Paris from the summit is breathtaking. At night, the tower sparkles with thousands of lights. Every evening, there is a light show that attracts tourists. The Eiffel Tower is made of iron and weighs about 10,100 tons. It was the tallest structure in the world until 1930. Today, it remains an architectural and engineering marvel. Millions of people visit the Eiffel Tower every year. It has three levels accessible to the public. Restaurants and souvenir shops are available on the first two levels. The third level is an observation deck. Tickets can be booked online to avoid long queues. The surrounding area, Champ de Mars, is perfect for picnics. Many romantic proposals happen near the Eiffel Tower. It also appears in countless movies and books. In short, visiting the Eiffel Tower is a must for anyone traveling to Paris.
